Who I Am

I'm Laurent Mahemba, a postgraduate student pursuing an MSc in IT (Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics) at Parul University, India. My focus is on offensive and defensive security — from penetration testing and network analysis to digital forensics and SOC operations.

I hold a BSc in Information Technology Application & Management from the State University of Zanzibar (SUZA), graduating with Upper Second Class Honours and a 4.0 GPA. My coursework included Computer Forensics & Cybersecurity, Network & Systems Administration, and Software Development.

Beyond cybersecurity, I have real-world professional experience as an ERP Functional Specialist at Kola Technologies and as a Google GDSC Club Lead, where I organised hackathons and developer workshops for 50+ participants.

I'm actively practising on TryHackMe, running a personal home lab, and working toward CompTIA Security+. Passionate about growing cybersecurity capacity across East Africa.
